Which visual style for the animated lessons?

Same beat — "the Moon orbits Earth and shines by reflecting sunlight" — shown three ways. They're live-animated. Click the one that feels right (the stage is the ~250px box inside the real lesson card).

🌙

A · Playful Emoji + Motion

Keeps today's friendly emoji look, but everything moves — Moon orbits, Earth spins, stars twinkle. Lightest to build, most on-brand with the current app.

B · Polished SVG Diagram

Clean vector "planetarium" scenes — drawn bodies, glow, dashed orbit paths, labels. Reads as real science, crisp at any size. Medium effort.

C · Cinematic / Immersive

Dark space, glowing gradient bodies, drifting star particles, the Moon's lit face morphing through phases. Biggest "wow", heaviest to build & tune.
